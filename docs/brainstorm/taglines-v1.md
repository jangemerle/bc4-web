# Kvalt — Tagline & Copy Brainstorm v1

**Date:** 2026-03-24
**Working customer personas:**

1. **Agency devs / freelancers** — Ship 5–15 client products a year using AI tools. Need each one to look different. Primary persona.
2. **Indie founders / solo SaaS builders** — Want their product to stand out in a sea of identical UIs. Secondary persona.
3. **Design-aware developers** — Know their UI is bland, lack the design skills to fix it. Tertiary persona.

**Core tension:** AI tools made everyone fast at producing the same thing. Speed without character is efficient mediocrity.

**Key differentiator:** Swap a config, get a completely different product personality — motion, typography, color, spatial rhythm. Not a reskin. A different soul.

## Hero block drafts

### Version A — Direct, benefit-led (agencies)
> **One system. Infinite faces.**
>
> Kvalt is an opinionated UI layer for AI-assisted development. Swap a config file and your next project looks, moves, and feels like a completely different product. Stop shipping the same interface in different colors.

### Version B — Problem-first (agencies)
> **Your last three projects look the same.**
>
> Not because you're lazy — because your tools are unopinionated. Kvalt gives AI-built products their own character: motion, typography, color, and spatial rhythm, all from a single token swap. Different client, different face, same speed.

### Version C — Provocative (universal)
> **AI gave everyone speed. Nobody got taste.**
>
> Kvalt is the opinionated design layer that turns generic AI output into products with character. One config changes the motion, the typography, the rhythm — everything that makes an interface feel like someone actually designed it.

### Version D — Aspiration (indie founders)
> **Your product isn't generic. Your UI shouldn't be either.**
>
> Kvalt gives your app a point of view — opinionated tokens, motion, and typography that make it feel intentional from the first click. No design team required. Just pick a face and ship.

### Version E — Short and sharp (universal)
> **Opinionated UI for the age of AI.**
>
> Design personality as a config file. Motion, typography, color, rhythm — swap the tokens, change the soul. Your tools move fast. Kvalt makes sure they move with taste.

## Raw word bank

Words that feel right: opinionated, faces, character, personality, soul, taste, intentional, configured, rhythm, motion, premium, bespoke, point of view, designed (vs. generated)

Words to avoid: beautiful (subjective, empty), modern (everyone says it), powerful (meaningless), revolutionary (cringe), seamless (overused)

## Open questions

2. **Kvalt or new name?** — If the product is "faces" / design personality configs, does it outgrow the Kvalt DS brand?
3. **Primary CTA** — "Browse faces" / "Try a face" / "See the difference" / "Pick your personality"?
4. **Do we lead with the AI angle or the differentiation angle?** — AI positioning is timely but could date quickly. Differentiation is evergreen.
