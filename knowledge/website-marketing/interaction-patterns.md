## Navigation Interactions

### Diagonal dropdown accommodation
**Source:** Equals
**What:** Dropdown menus have a 300ms close delay that accommodates diagonal mouse movement from trigger to submenu. Without this, moving diagonally causes the dropdown to close prematurely.
**Why it matters:** Invisible UX polish that separates good from great. Users never notice it working, but they notice when it's missing.
**Kvalt application:** Implement on any dropdown/flyout navigation.
