## Launch day essentials
1. **Hunter** — find someone with large PH following to hunt Kvalt. One good hunter > 500 followers
2. **Launch assets** — thumbnail, tagline, first 3 screenshots decide everything. Kvalt's visual polish is a genuine advantage here
3. **Timing** — Tuesday-Thursday, avoid days with big-name launches
4. **External push** — X/Twitter, Discord communities, email list. First 2 hours set the trajectory
5. **Tagline** — needs to be sharp and differentiated. Not "a design system" — something like the personality/face angle

## Higher-leverage parallel moves
- Build in public on X/Twitter (design decisions, character system, motion). This compounds; PH followers don't
- Get into 2-3 dev/design Discords and be helpful (not promotional)
- Ship free faces on GitHub BEFORE PH launch — so launch day has social proof ("X stars, used by Y projects")

## Key insight
PH is a great amplifier but a bad foundation. Use it as a launch event, not an audience-building platform. Content leads cost half as much and convert 2x better than advertising (Priestley). Invest in compounding assets (GitHub stars, Twitter following, community reputation) over PH follower count.
