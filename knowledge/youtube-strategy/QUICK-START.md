# YouTube for Kvalt — Quick Start Reference

**Complete research:** See `youtube-for-design-systems.md`

---

## TL;DR

**Posting:** 2-3 videos/week (consistency beats daily uploads)
**Format:** 7-15 min tutorials + 4-5 min process/decision videos
**Title:** 40-60 chars, keyword first, one power word, parenthetical signal
**Thumbnail:** 0-3 words, high contrast, show emotion (CTR 4-6% avg → 7%+ good)
**Monetization:** At 100K subs: $800-3K/video sponsorships + product sales
**Growth:** Channels posting 12+ videos/month grow 8x faster in views, 3x faster in subs

## Best Performing Title Patterns

```
[Lead + First 45 chars for mobile]
[Bracket or parenthetical for secondary signal]

Examples:
"Design Systems Done Right: 5 Patterns [Figma]"
"The Design Decision Behind Kvalt (Complete Guide)"
"Building Components in 10 Minutes (No Boilerplate)"
"Why We Chose These Colors: Design Tokens Explained"
```

**Power words (use 1-2 max):**
- Proven, Secret, Essential, Critical, Complete, Fast

---

## Thumbnail Formula

**Word Count:** 0-3 words max (mobile constraint = 60% of views)
**Design:** High contrast + emotion/face (20-30% CTR boost from faces)
**Layout:** Consider before/after split-screen (boosted one tech channel from 3.2% → 5.8% CTR)

**Test:** Use YouTube's native Test & Compare feature to A/B thumbnails

## Video Length Sweet Spots

| Type | Length | Retention Target | Content |
|------|--------|------------------|---------|
| **Format** | 4-5 min | 70%+ | Quick showcase, why this choice |
| **Tutorial** | 10-15 min | 50%+ | Setup, implementation, examples |
| **Deep Dive** | 20-30 min | 45%+ | Design process, decision rationale |

**Rule:** 10 min at 60% retention > 25 min at 40% retention

---

## Kvalt's Unique Angle

❌ Don't: Make generic "how to build a design system" tutorials
✅ Do: Show "why we made this choice" + the thinking behind it

This differentiates from 90% of design content (which is tool tutorials). Builds authority faster.

**Example video structure:**
1. Problem/constraint
2. Explored 2-3 options (show the thinking)
3. Why we chose one direction
4. Implementation demo
5. Share the artifact/config

## What to Avoid

❌ Daily uploads (kills retention focus)
❌ Generic tutorials (50K+ already exist)
❌ Titles without specificity or emotion
❌ Thumbnails with 5+ words (mobile death)
❌ Videos under 50% average view duration
❌ Posting without consistency

---

## Analytics to Track

1. **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** 4-6% avg, 7%+ good, 10%+ excellent
2. **Average View Duration:** Target 50%+ (more important than length)
3. **Audience Retention Graph:** Look for drop-off points
4. **Comments/1000 views:** 2-3 avg, 4-6 good, 7+ exceptional
5. **Watch Time:** Hours watched (YouTube's ranking signal)

**Dashboard:** Set up vidIQ or TubeBuddy for ongoing keyword + competitor research
